THEME PARK

THE AGE OF DISCOVERY

JOIN THE SEARCH TO FATHOM THE WORLD'S DEADLIEST ENIGMA

The Age of Discovery is a theme park inspired by a historical period of European global exploration that extended from the early 15th century to 18th century. The Age of Discovery is divided into five sections namely: Fantasy land, Skull island, Devil’s Sea, Ancient China and an Educational Section consisting of a Museum and Show. In each of these sections are different small sections such as The Chinese Garden and The Wheal Port. The Age Of Discovery is a Family adventure park , promising to fill everyone’s day with exciting ride adventures
